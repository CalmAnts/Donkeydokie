About Us

DonkeyDokie is a comprehensive platform for college students to find their travel compaions. Here you can post trips, choose your own compaions and apply for other students' trips. You can also maintain and manage your trips by recording your experience and tracking your bills. It is an ideal way to get acquainted with college students around the world by sharing your travel notes with others and exchaging your ideas.
